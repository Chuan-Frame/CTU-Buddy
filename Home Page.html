Welcome to CTU-Buddy

An Educational System that enhances the learning experience for students.

Key Features

This website allows students to:

Timetables

The Timetables feature on CTU-Buddy helps students effectively manage their time by organizing classes, assignments, and activities in one place.
This tool is designed to enhance productivity, reduce stress, and support academic success.

Discussion

It provides a platform for students to engage in collaborative problem-solving and knowledge sharing.
This feature helps students enhance their understanding, develop critical thinking, and improve communication skills by participating in academic discussions.

Share Resources

Students and educators can easily upload, download, and share educational materials in various formats.
Secure access and privacy measures ensure safe sharing, creating a supportive academic environment.
